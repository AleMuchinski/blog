Who am I?

I am Brazilian, 24 years old, and come from a family of teachers in the city of Curitiba. Currently, I am living in Denmark making one of my dreams of studying abroad happen, also, I am working as a data and analytics consultant teaching and building several data tools for many companies. As one of my personal projects, I help brazilian students that just like me, could not afford to study abroad. I believe that it is my responsibility to give back the opportunities that were provided to me in life, and I try to do that through the an NGO called BRASA.

My area of focus lies in designing dashboards using Tableau and Power BI, data transformation (ETL) through Alteryx and Knime and statistical data analysis with Python and R. I enjoy coding as much as talking with users to iterate and improve the experiences that reach business goals, after all it doesn't matter how complete a tool you have if it doesn't bring value to you.
